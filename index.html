Our Story

Born from a
pitch competition.

The Paddock Society didn't start in a garage — it started on a stage. In November 2025, Eduardo Mejia stood in front of judges at ACC's NEST FEST student entrepreneurship competition with an idea: Austin's car culture deserved its own elevated event brand.


Together with co-founder Jose Varela, they turned that pitch into reality in under four months. The Paddock Premiere is their first event — and the beginning of something much larger.

Two Founders. One Vision.
